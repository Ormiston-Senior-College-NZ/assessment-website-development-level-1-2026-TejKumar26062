Club of Indian Diaspora

About Us

We are the Club of Indian Diaspora in Ormiston Senior College. We aim to connect and support individuals of Indian origin of the local community. Our club provides a platform for members to share their experiences, culture, and traditions, while also fostering a sense of community and belonging. We organize various events and activities throughout the year, including cultural festivals, networking opportunities, and social gatherings. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members, regardless of their background or location.

The Values we Uphold


We are committed to fostering a sense of community and belonging among individuals of Indian origin in Ormiston Senior College. We value diversity and inclusivity, and strive to create a welcoming environment for all members. We believe in celebrating our culture and traditions, while also embracing new ideas and perspectives. We are dedicated to supporting our members and providing opportunities for personal growth and development. We are passionate about making a positive impact in our local community and beyond.
