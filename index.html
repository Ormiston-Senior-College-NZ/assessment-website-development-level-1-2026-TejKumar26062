Club of Indian Diaspora

About Us

We are the Club of Indian Diaspora in Ormiston Senior College. We aim to connect and support individuals of Indian origin of the local community. Our club provides a platform for members to share their experiences, culture, and traditions, while also fostering a sense of community and belonging. We organize various events and activities throughout the year, including cultural festivals, networking opportunities, and social gatherings. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all members, regardless of their background or location.

The Values we Uphold

+

The Club of Indian Diaspora at Ormiston Senior College is dedicated to creating a strong sense of community and belonging for students of Indian origin. Our club reflects the school values of Papatūānuku and Waka, which guide the way we support and connect with one another. Through Papatūānuku, we value and care for our cultural and social identities by celebrating Indian traditions, heritage, and diversity within our school community. Through Waka, we encourage members to contribute actively as part of the wider Ormiston Senior College community. By embracing both tradition and new perspectives, we aim to support personal growth, strengthen connections, and make a positive impact within our school and beyond.
